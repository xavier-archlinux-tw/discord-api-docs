## Multiplayer Experiences in Discord

Activities are multiplayer games and social experiences embedded directly in Discord. They can be launched in channels, DMs, or from the App Launcher with no external window or separate download required. 

Players can jump in together with friends already in a voice channel, making Activities a natural fit for party games, co-watching, collaborative tools, and more.

![Discord Activities](/images/platform/activity-playing.png)

## Discord 中的多人體驗 (Multiplayer Experiences in Discord)

互動活動 (Activities) 是直接嵌入在 Discord 中的多人遊戲和社交體驗。它們可以在頻道、私訊 (DM) 中啟動，或者從應用程式啟動器 (App Launcher) 啟動，不需要外部視窗或單獨下載。

玩家可以與已經在語音頻道中的好友一起加入，這使得互動活動非常適合派對遊戲、共同觀看、協作工具等。

![Discord 互動活動](/images/platform/activity-playing.png)

## Embedded App SDK for Activities

Under the hood, Activities are an iframe that communicates with Discord using the [Embedded App SDK](/developers/en-us/developer-tools/embedded-app-sdk). The SDK provides methods for interacting between your Activity and the Discord client.
</div>

## 用於互動活動的嵌入式應用程式 SDK (Embedded App SDK for Activities)

在底層，互動活動是一個透過 [嵌入式應用程式 SDK (Embedded App SDK)](/developers/bilingual/developer-tools/embedded-app-sdk) 與 Discord 進行通訊的 iframe。該 SDK 提供了在您的互動活動與 Discord 用戶端之間進行互動的方法。
