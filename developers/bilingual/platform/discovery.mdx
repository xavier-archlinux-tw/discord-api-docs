## App Directory

The [App Directory](https://discord.com/discovery/applications) is a searchable hub where users can browse apps by name, category, or collection, and view a full product page with descriptions, screenshots, and links.

To appear here, your app needs to be verified and opted into discovery. See [Enabling Discovery](/developers/en-us/discovery/enabling-discovery) for the step-by-step setup.
</div>
<div>

## 應用程式目錄 (App Directory)

[應用程式目錄 (App Directory)](https://discord.com/discovery/applications) 是一個可搜尋的樞紐，使用者可以透過名稱、類別或精選特輯瀏覽應用程式，並查看包含說明、螢幕截圖與連結的完整產品頁面。

要出現在這裡，您的應用程式需要經過驗證並加入探索功能。請參閱 [啟用探索功能](/developers/bilingual/discovery/enabling-discovery) 以了解逐步設定方法。
</div>

## Organic Discovery & Sharing

Once your app is discoverable, social interactions within Discord become a natural acquisition channel. When users interact with your app in a server, others in that server can see it and visit your app's profile to install it themselves. 

Each Discord app also has sharable links that take users to your app's profile or [store page](/developers/en-us/monetization/managing-skus#linking-to-your-store), shareable anywhere from Discord messages to external websites.
</div>
<div>
    <div style={{ borderBottom: "2px solid #5865f2", paddingBottom: "8px", marginBottom: "16px", fontWeight: "bold", fontSize: "18px" }}>🇹🇼 繁體中文</div>

## 自然探索與分享 (Organic Discovery & Sharing)

一旦您的應用程式可被探索，Discord 內部的社群互動就會成為一個自然的獲取管道。當使用者在伺服器中與您的應用程式互動時，該伺服器中的其他使用者可以看到它，並存取您應用程式的檔案頁面以自行安裝。

每個 Discord 應用程式也都擁有可分享的連結，可以帶領使用者前往您的應用程式檔案頁面或 [商店頁面](/developers/bilingual/monetization/managing-skus#linking-to-your-store)，這些連結可在從 Discord 訊息到外部網站的任何地方進行分享。
