## Setting Up Activity Metadata

Navigate to `Settings -> General Information` to update:
- **Application Name**: Publicly visible name
- **Application Icon**: Publicly visible icon
- **Application Description**: Shown in the Activity Shelf
- **Max Participants**: Maximum number of players

## Setting Up Activity Art Assets

Navigate to [Activities -> Art Assets](https://discord.com/developers/applications/select/embedded/assets) to configure:

- **Embedded Background**: 16:9, at least 1024px wide
- **Cover Art**: 16:9 and 13:11, at least 1024px wide
- **Video Preview**: 640x360, mp4, under 10s, under 1MB

## 設定互動活動中繼資料 (Setting Up Activity Metadata)

前往 `Settings -> General Information` 進行更新：
- **應用程式名稱 (Application Name)**：公開顯示的名稱
- **應用程式圖示 (Application Icon)**：公開顯示的圖示
- **應用程式說明 (Application Description)**：顯示在互動活動書架中
- **最多參與人數 (Max Participants)**：最大玩家數量

## 設定互動活動美術素材 (Setting Up Activity Art Assets)

前往 [Activities -> Art Assets](https://discord.com/developers/applications/select/embedded/assets) 進行設定：

- **嵌入式背景 (Embedded Background)**：16:9，寬度至少 1024 像素
- **封面圖 (Cover Art)**：16:9 和 13:11，寬度至少 1024 像素
- **影片預覽 (Video Preview)**：640x360，mp4，10 秒內，1MB 以內
