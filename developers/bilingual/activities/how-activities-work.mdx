Activities are web applications that run in an iframe within Discord on desktop, mobile and web. In order to achieve this, we use the [`postMessage`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/Window/postMessage) protocol to enable secure communication between your application and Discord.

The [Embedded App SDK](https://github.com/discord/embedded-app-sdk) simplifies this process by managing the `postMessage` protocol on your behalf. For details on available commands and their usage, consult the [SDK Reference](/developers/en-us/developer-tools/embedded-app-sdk).

互動活動 (Activities) 是託管在 iframe 中，並在電腦版、行動版與網頁版 Discord 中執行的網頁應用程式。為實現此目的，我們使用 [postMessage](https://developer.mozilla.org/zh-TW/docs/Web/API/Window/postMessage) 協定在您的應用程式與 Discord 之間進行安全通訊。

## Launching Activities

After you have Activities enabled in your Application's [Activity's settings](https://discord.com/developers/applications/select/embedded/settings), your app can launch Activities in two ways:
1. When a user invokes your app's Entry Point command in the App Launcher
2. By responding to an interaction with the `LAUNCH_ACTIVITY` callback type

## 啟動互動活動

在您的應用程式 [互動活動設定](https://discord.com/developers/applications/select/embedded/settings) 中啟用互動活動之後，您的 App 可以透過以下兩種方式啟動互動活動：
1. 當使用者在應用程式啟動器 (App Launcher) 中呼叫您 App 的進入點指令 (Entry Point Command) 時。
2. 透過回傳類型為 `LAUNCH_ACTIVITY` (類型為 `12`) 的互動回應。

## Activity Lifecycle

1. **Initialization:** When your iframe is loaded, it will include unique query parameters in its URL.
2. **Handshake Process:** Constructing the SDK instance begins a handshake process. The `ready()` method resolves once a connection is established.
3. **Authorization and Authentication:** Acquire necessary permissions (scopes).
4. **Interacting with Discord Client:** Subscribe to events and send commands.
5. **Disconnection and Errors:** Close payload signals connection termination.

## 互動活動生命週期

1. **初始化：** 當您的 iframe 被載入至 Discord 中時，其網址將會包含獨特的查詢參數。
2. **握手流程：** 建立 SDK 執行個體會開始與 Discord 用戶端的握手流程。`ready()` 方法在成功建立連線後即會 resolve。
3. **授權與身分驗證：** 身分驗證以獲取必要的權限 (Scopes)。
4. **與 Discord 用戶端互動：** 驗證完成後，訂閱事件並發送指令。
5. **中斷連線與錯誤：** 接收或發送 CLOSE 負載代表發生錯誤或需要結束連線。
