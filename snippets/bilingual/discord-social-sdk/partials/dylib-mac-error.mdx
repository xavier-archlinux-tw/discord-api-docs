<Accordion title="Mac libdiscord_partner_sdk.dylib Not Opened">
On Mac you may get the error "libdiscord_partner_sdk.dylib" Not Opened because Apple couldn't verify it. If this happens press **Done** on the popup.

![Error](/images/social-sdk/getting-started/partials/error.png)

You'll need to open your **System Settings > Privacy & Security** and scroll down to the **Security** section. It will tell you "libdiscord_partner_sdk.dylib" was blocked to protect your Mac. Press **Open Anyway** and try running again.

![Settings](/images/social-sdk/getting-started/partials/security-settings.png)

Now when you get the pop up you'll have the option to select **Open Anyway** and it will be able to use it successfully.

![Open](/images/social-sdk/getting-started/partials/open-anyway.png)

<Accordion title="Mac 版 libdiscord_partner_sdk.dylib 無法開啟">
在 Mac 上，您可能會遇到因為 Apple 無法驗證該檔案而導致「libdiscord_partner_sdk.dylib」無法開啟的錯誤。如果發生此情況，請在彈出視窗上按下 **完成 (Done)**。

![錯誤](/images/social-sdk/getting-started/partials/error.png)

您需要開啟您的 **系統設定 > 隱私權與安全性**，並向下捲動到 **安全性** 區塊。它會告訴您「libdiscord_partner_sdk.dylib」已被封鎖以保護您的 Mac。請按下 **強制開啟 (Open Anyway)** 並嘗試重新執行。

![設定](/images/social-sdk/getting-started/partials/security-settings.png)

現在，當彈出視窗再次出現時，您將有選項可以選擇 **強制開啟 (Open Anyway)**，接著便能成功使用它。

![開啟](/images/social-sdk/getting-started/partials/open-anyway.png)
