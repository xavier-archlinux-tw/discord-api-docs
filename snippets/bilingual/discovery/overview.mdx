## App Directory

Once you've [enabled discovery for your app](/developers/en-us/discovery/enabling-discovery), users can find it in the App Directory — a searchable hub where they can browse by name, category, or collection.

- **Search**: Users can search for your app by name and install it.
- **App Directory Product Page**: Share information about your app, including descriptions, images, videos, and links.

![Your Discord App Profile](/images/discovery/directory-product-page.png)

## 應用程式目錄 (App Directory)

一旦您為您的應用程式 [啟用了探索功能](/developers/bilingual/discovery/enabling-discovery)，使用者就可以在「應用程式目錄」中找到它 —— 這是一個可搜尋的樞紐，使用者可以透過名稱、類別或精選特輯進行瀏覽。

- **搜尋 (Search)**：使用者可以透過名稱搜尋您的應用程式並安裝它。
- **應用程式目錄產品頁面 (App Directory Product Page)**：分享有關您應用程式的資訊，包括說明、圖片、影片與連結。

## App Launcher

The App Launcher lets users discover apps through collections and search from the app shapes icon <Icon icon="AppsIcon" /> throughout Discord.

![App Launcher in Discord](/images/discovery/app-launcher.png)

### App Launcher Collections

- **Recent Apps**: Apps you've recently used or installed will appear at the top of the App Launcher
- **Installed Apps**: Apps already added to your account or server (if applicable)
- **Curated Collections**: A mix of user favorites, staff picks, and recommended apps
- **Partner Apps**: Some apps may be developed in collaboration with Discord, marked with a "Partner" tag
- **Promoted Apps**: Apps given more visibility in the App Launcher or App Directory, identified by a "Promoted" tag

Search results are ranked based on relevance to the query and popularity based on usage. Apps that use ads or offer in-app purchases are marked with "Ad-supported" and "In-App Purchases" tags on their details page.

### Editorial Recommendations

Apps can be featured in the App Directory or App Launcher as part of a curated collection or promotion. Discord's editorial team will reach out to you if we are interested in featuring your app.

## 應用程式啟動器 (App Launcher)

應用程式啟動器可讓使用者透過 Discord 各處的應用程式形狀圖示 <Icon icon="AppsIcon" /> 來搜尋與瀏覽應用程式特輯。

![Discord 中的應用程式啟動器](/images/discovery/app-launcher.png)

### 應用程式啟動器特輯 (App Launcher Collections)

- **最近使用的 App (Recent Apps)**：您最近使用或安裝的應用程式將會顯示在應用程式啟動器的最上方
- **已安裝的 App (Installed Apps)**：已加入到您帳戶或伺服器的應用程式（如適用）
- **精選特輯 (Curated Collections)**：使用者最愛、官方推薦和精選推薦應用程式的組合
- **合作夥伴 App (Partner Apps)**：某些與 Discord 合作開發的應用程式，標有「Partner (合作夥伴)」標籤
- **推廣 App (Promoted Apps)**：在應用程式啟動器或應用程式目錄中獲得更高曝光率的應用程式，標有「Promoted (推廣)」標籤

搜尋結果是根據與查詢的關聯性，以及基於使用量的受歡迎程度進行排名的。使用廣告或提供應用程式內購買的應用程式，將在其詳細資訊頁面上標記為「Ad-supported (包含廣告)」和「In-App Purchases (程式內購買)」。

### 編輯推薦 (Editorial Recommendations)

應用程式可以作為精選特輯或推廣的一部分，在應用程式目錄或應用程式啟動器中被推薦。如果我們對推薦您的應用程式感興趣，Discord 的編輯團隊將會主動與您聯絡。

## Social Discovery

Once your app is discoverable, users may find it through social interactions in Discord. When your app is used in a server, it may be visible to other users, allowing them to learn more about it and install it themselves from your app's profile.

### Sharing Links

Create shareable links to your app's profile page, store page, or specific items. Share these links in Discord, emails, or on your website.

![A shared embed link in Discord for the Sandscape app](/images/discovery/sharing-links.png)

### Rich Presence

Use [Rich Presence](/developers/en-us/rich-presence/overview) to show what users are doing in your app, driving more users to discover it.

![Examples of Rich Presence data on Discord user profiles](/images/rich-presence/examples.png)

## 社群探索 (Social Discovery)

一旦您的應用程式可被探索，使用者就可以透過 Discord 中的社群互動找到它。當您的應用程式在伺服器中使用時，它可能對其他使用者可見，從而讓他們了解更多資訊並從您的應用程式設定檔中自行安裝。

### 分享連結 (Sharing Links)

建立指向您應用程式個人資料頁面、商店頁面或特定項目的可分享連結。在 Discord、電子郵件或您的網站上分享這些連結。

![Discord 中 Sandscape 應用程式的可分享嵌入連結](/images/discovery/sharing-links.png)

### 豐富狀態 (Rich Presence)

使用 [豐富狀態 (Rich Presence)](/developers/bilingual/rich-presence/overview) 顯示使用者在您的應用程式中正在做什麼，進而吸引更多使用者去探索它。
